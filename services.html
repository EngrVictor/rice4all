Grow Naturally Leaders In The Field

In our Rice plantation, we make sure that our Consumers eat a Rice that is well nutritious and not polished because polished Rice is not as nutritious as Rice from which only the Hulls or Husk are removed. We achieved this by making sure that parboiling is done before hulling. It is the process of steaming and drying Grains before hulling.

Parboiling has the following advantages

01

It toughens the Rice and therefore prevent breakages before when you hull.

02

It makes the Hull easier to remove.

03

It causes the Vitamins in the outer layer usually removed to migrate deeper into the grains and are not lost during milling.

Experience the real agriculture

Nigerian Rice have been analysed and shown to be nutritious than the foreign Rice due to its processing methods and our soil is the best for Agricultural produces. It is shown to have more of Vitamins than the foreign Rice like the Thiamine which is Vitamin B₁. With the current ban on Rice importation in Nigeria and the rapid growth of Rice farming in states like Kebbi has become somewhat bearable, but Nigerians keep wondering why Rice cannot be affordable as a basic meal can be. What is true situation of Rice in Nigerian? How much does it cost? In Nigeria, Rice is not only a daily meal that can be taken anywhere and anytime, but it is a common feature at Parties and Ceremonies, including Birthdays Marriages, naming Ceremonies, and burial rites.

Rice for all is a group of private sector that comes together to make the food crop “Rice” available to the consumers so that Nigerians can appreciate and patronize our locally Produced rice.
